About Prava

A festival built by students, for the curious.

PRAVA is our college's flagship annual technical and cultural fest — a three-day convergence of engineers, artists, coders, and performers. It began as a single-day coding meet and has grown into a national gathering that pulls in participants from over fifty campuses.

MissionGive every student a stage to build, perform, and compete.
VisionBecome India's most-loved campus fest by 2030.
Why AttendReal prize money, real mentors, real portfolio wins.
ExperienceConcerts, food trucks, and all-night hack rooms.
