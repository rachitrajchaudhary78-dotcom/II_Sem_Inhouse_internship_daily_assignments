About PRAVAH

A festival engineered for innovation—and designed for delight.

PRAVAH blends high-signal tech competitions with cultural showcases, bringing together creators from across colleges to learn, compete, perform, and network.

Mission

Empower students with real-world challenges, mentorship, and a platform to showcase excellence.

Vision

Build a future-facing festival culture with global-grade experiences and community.

Why Attend

Win prizes, earn certificates, meet teams, and unlock opportunities with our sponsor ecosystem.

Experience
